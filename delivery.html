Delivery Terms and Conditions

1. Delivery Services

We offer delivery services for all orders made on our website. Our delivery service options include:

  • Doorstep delivery: This is our standard delivery service for our customers in Male’ and Hulhumale. We normally deliver within same day.
  • Boat delivery: We deliver to boats and ferries for our customers who are based in the islands.
  • Resort delivery: This option is for our customers from the resorts. We arrange delivery to resort speedboats, supply boats & Male’ office.
  • Call for delivery: This is for those customers who are not sure where they will be during our delivery times, we will deliver to your location after giving you a call.

2. Delivery Charges

We offer free deliveries for all orders above 500rf, while deliveries below this amount can incur a delivery fee.

We reserve the right to include any charges (if any) for the deliveries that might include boat fees or pickup/car charges. The customer will be informed prior to delivery.

3. Delivery Schedule

Delivery times are estimated. We will do our best to deliver your order within the estimated time slot and ensure timely service.
